Where we get our data

Artificial Analysis Image Arena

Weight: 35%

Blind A/B comparison arena. Users vote on which image they prefer without knowing which model generated it. Produces ELO ratings based on thousands of votes.

LM Arena (Text-to-Image)

Weight: 30%

Formerly LMSYS Chatbot Arena. Large-scale crowdsourced blind comparisons. The gold standard for community preference ranking.

LLM Stats

Weight: 15%

Aggregator that compiles rankings from multiple independent leaderboards into a unified cross-source view.

Expert Reviews

Weight: 20%

Our editorial assessment combining hands-on testing, professional review consensus, and community sentiment from Reddit, X, and Discord.
