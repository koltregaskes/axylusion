Hello, and welcome to Axy Lusion.

This site is my creative home for exploring what happens when human direction meets AI tools. It pulls together the work, experiments, and references that sit around that process rather than treating each image or track like it appeared out of nowhere.

## What is on this website

- **Gallery** for the visual work, including cinematic portraits, fantasy scenes, darker moods, and more experimental pieces.
- **Videos** for motion work and AI video tests.
- **Music** for tracks and sound-led experiments.
- **News** for useful updates from the creative AI world.
- **Tools** for the platforms and software shaping the workflow.
- **A-List** for standout links, references, and signal worth keeping an eye on.
- **About** for the person behind the prompts.

## Why this exists

I wanted one place that shows the bigger picture: the outputs, the tools, the discoveries, and the direction of travel. AI creativity moves quickly, and a lot of what gets posted online disappears into timelines. This site is a way to keep the strongest work and the most useful context together.

## What comes next

This blog will be a space for:

- welcome notes and project updates
- behind-the-scenes process posts
- thoughts on tools and creative workflows
- occasional context around new releases and experiments

Thanks for visiting. Have a look around, and start anywhere that feels interesting.
